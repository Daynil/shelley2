Cassie

My name is Cassie and I am a very special dog. I was adopted from Rover Rescue in 2012 and after a year of training, I became certified to go to work with my mom and help people who have been through difficult life events.
My mom chose to adopt me because I know what it’s like to heal from trauma. I’m living proof that you can trust, love life, and heal from those painful memories and emotions. People like to have me in session for many different reasons. Petting me helps lower anxiety. Some people feel more safe and find it easier to talk with me in the room. Some people need help with certain goals, like learning assertiveness or strengthening self esteem, and I am used in various activities to help you accomplish those goals. I'm a very good listener and no matter what you have been through, I don't judge and I love everyone who walks in the door!
When I’m not at work, some of my favorite things are: going on walks, chasing rabbits and chipmunks, napping, sniffing everything, and playing with my kong.
If you would like to meet me, learn more about me, or have me in your sessions, contact my mom for more information.
