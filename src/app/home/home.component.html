Empowerment on the journey to healing

Hello! Thank you for visiting my webpage.

My specialty is helping people heal from trauma and grief. I like to create a safe environment and use a collaborative approach, because I believe it is important that you have a say in your healing journey. Depending on your needs, I enjoy using different modalities such as Art Therapy, Sand Tray Therapy, Animal Assisted Therapy, and Mindfulness in addition to talk therapy with individuals, couples, and families.

If you feel that it is the right time for you to start your healing journey, please feel free to contact me. I look forward to talking with you soon!
